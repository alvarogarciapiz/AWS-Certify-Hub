- Potential impact

## Response Times

- We aim to respond to security reports within **48 hours**.
- A fix or mitigation will be released as soon as possible, typically within **7 days** for critical issues.

## Disclosure Policy

- Please do **not** disclose vulnerabilities publicly until they have been addressed.
- We will acknowledge your report and credit you (if desired) in the release notes.

Thank you for helping keep AWS P2 secure!
